Finite Fields and Fixed Points of functions going up and down

We consider piecewise linear functions f(x) going up and down p times. The fixed points of fⁿ are in bijection with elements of the finite field with pⁿ elements, where orbits under f are in correspondence with irreducible polynomials over the field.

This is a joint work of Julián Pulido y Emerson León. More details of these bijections can be found here.
